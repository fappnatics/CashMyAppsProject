Introduction

1. What is CashMaker?

CashMaker is an app that allows you to make money and earn rewards downloading apps.

2. Can my device run CashMaker?

System Requirements: Android 4.0.3 or higher.

Apps

1. I downloaded an app from CashMaker but I didn’t get any Coins!

1. After downloading an app you need to run it and interact with it (any action possible) to be sure that you have correctly installed it.
2. If you have downloaded the app in the past and you are reinstalling it now through CashMaker you won’t be able to earn any Coins, you can only earn Coins for trying an app for the first time, not for reinstalling it.
3. Please bear in mind that the Coins won’t show up instantly in your CashMaker account, there is always a delay between installing an app and adding the coins in your CashMaker account.

If after reading this you still have questions, doubts or technical difficulties, please contact using the email address linked to your CashMaker account to: info@cashmakertheapp.com.

2. Some of the apps in CashMaker can’t be used on my device! How can I earn the Coins offered by those apps?

Not every app on CashMaker will be usable on every platform or device. We apologize for the inconvenience.

Money & Gift Cards

1. How do I earn gift cards or money?

If you have enough Coins, you can exchange them with money or gift cards in the withdraw option. For security reasons, there is a 12-hours waiting period after money or gift cards are sent.

2. Can I get a Credit refund from unused gift cards?

Sorry. Gift cards claimed with Coins aren’t eligible for refunds.

3. How can I receive money from the Coins earned?

Just go to the Withdraw option and you will be able to see that you can convert your Coins into money and send them to your PayPal account.

Troubleshooting

1. The app is loading very slow.

Try to use CashMaker over a WiFI connection it will be faster, issues could happen with 3G and 4G connections.

2. I saw a cool app with a really interesting reward but just for a few days

Please bear in mind that some of the offers will be only available for a limited period of time.
